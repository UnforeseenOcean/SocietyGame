It’s time for Quarterly Reports!

How did your sector do? Is everyone a team player? …No?

Well, now’s your chance to prove it! You can investigate the teammate you find most suspicious to see if they really have been choosing to be selfish. You can place a bet on this player’s selfishness, and if you are correct, you get some of the player’s points and your own points. If you falsely accuse them, then you win nothing.
