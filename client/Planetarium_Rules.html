Rules

It is your duty as a citizen of the world to keep Earth alive, and your duty to your nation to help it thrive.

You must compete against the other nations and stand out in your own sector by any means necessary. But beware! All your choices have consequences.

Each decision you make will impact your world's score, your nation's score, your sector's score, and your individual score.

Example:

Extort families in the Warzone

This choice has the following effect:

Individual +2 pts; Military (Group) -2 pts; World -1 pt

In order to win, you must help your nation become the best one, have maintained your sector at a satisfactory level of 20 points or higher, and be the highest scorer in your sector. Your nation's score is the sum of your nation's sector scores, so helping your sector helps you, too.

Of course, all of this is irrelevant if you let the world die because its score reached 0!

So remember: your choices will determine the fate of not only your nation, but your world!
